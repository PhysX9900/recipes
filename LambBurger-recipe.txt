Ingredients:
 - 1 pound ground lamb
 - 1 beefsteak tomato, sliced
 - 1 cup of sharp gouda cheese, shredded
 - 2 tablespoons black pepper
 - 1 head of romain lettuce
 - 1 pack of brioche buns (6)
 - 1 bottle of tzatziki sauce
 - 1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green), chopped into strips

Directions:
 1. Mix shredded cheese into the ground lamb.
 2. Shape lamb into 3-4 patties and fry until desired rareness.
 3. In separate pan, fry peppers until they are tender. 
 4. Toast the brioche buns in the oven at 350 F for about 10-15 minutes.
 5. Place cooked burgers into buns, add lettuce, tomato slices, fryed peppers, and tzatziki sauce.
